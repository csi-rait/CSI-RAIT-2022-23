Induction of FEs

22-08-2022



The new era of CSI-RAIT began with an interactive session conducted by the team members for FE students. The session opened with the core members formally introducing themselves as well as the committee to the audience. Thereafter, an immersive and participatory session on a prevalent topic - ‘ Web Design and Development ’ was carried out by the team. The main highlight of this session was to make students understand the basics of web development and its potential in the technological industry. Students were also taught some principal elements of HTML and CSS, and the techniques behind the creation of websites.
